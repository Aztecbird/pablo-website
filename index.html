ABOUT PABLO

A Lifetime Dedicated to Healing Through Music

Born in Mexico, I began my musical journey at the age of four, discovering the profound power of piano keys to express emotions words cannot capture. My path led me to the prestigious New England Conservatory of Music, where I deepened my understanding of composition and the art of creating atmosphere through sound.

Over three decades, I have produced more than 50 albums of healing music, composed scores for films, and touched the lives of millions through platforms like Spotify, YouTube, and Insight Timer. My music is crafted with one purpose: to guide listeners toward inner peace, relaxation, and self-discovery.

Today, based in Spain, I continue to create music that serves as a companion for meditation, sleep, stress relief, and those precious moments of stillness we all deserve.
